🧠 Classical Neuroendocrine Signaling Models
--------------------------------------------

Government and educational data map stress hormone transit pathways via non-digital molecular cascades:

-   HPA Axis Cascade: Stress triggers hypothalamic CRH, initiating anterior pituitary ACTH release.
-   Adrenal Synthesis: ACTH prompts the adrenal cortex to secrete cortisol.
-   Receptor Translocation: Cortisol binds cytoplasmic glucocorticoid receptors to regulate gene translation.
-   Feedback Modulation: Systems rely on continuous pharmacodynamic loops rather than binary/quaternary logic.
-   Biliary Anatomy: The cystic duct structurally manages mechanical bile flow dynamics. [3, 5, 11]
